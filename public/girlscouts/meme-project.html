Meme Project

In this project, we're going to make an animated, interactive meme! It can just be funny or have an important message.

1) First you need to get the base images for your meme project. You can either remix this project or find your own images online to import into your project.

2) Then add some looping motion to your sprites - jumping up and down, wiggling, tilting, or any other kind of motion you want. Experiment with using different kinds of motion with different kinds of loops!

3) Next, add some words to your meme. You can create a sprite with some text or you can use the Text to Speech extension. Or you can use both!

3) Finally, add some interaction to your meme. You can use a keyboard press or the mouse in many different ways. Be creative!
